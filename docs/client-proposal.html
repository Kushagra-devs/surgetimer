1. Executive Summary

TopicDetails
Project ObjectiveDesign and deliver a production-oriented horse show jumping timing platform integrated with ALGE hardware, vMix broadcast workflows, spectator live scoreboards, and real-time operational dashboards.
Business NeedReplace fragmented timing and display operations with a stable, server-authoritative software system that supports judges, operators, broadcasters, and spectators from a unified control platform.
Primary OutcomeA reliable competition operations system with live timing, manual override controls, full event history, hardware diagnostics, overlays, desktop widgets, and mobile-friendly spectator views.

Requirement AreaClient NeedProposed Response

ReliabilitySoftware must remain usable even during hardware or UI disruptions.Realtime backend state, manual fallback path, diagnostics, logs, and operational recovery workflows.
